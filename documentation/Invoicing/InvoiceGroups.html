    Invoice Groups

    General Information

    An invoice group can be stored for every user, invoice type, and invoice batch. When an invoice group is assigned to users, then those users only see the invoice types and invoice batches associated with that invoice group.

    On the invoice group card, users can determine down to the field which filter definitions each member in this group is allowed to use or not.

    This makes it possible to limit the invoice options for certain users (e.g. to certain sites, order codes, jobs, etc.).
